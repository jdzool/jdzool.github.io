Data Dojo: Design of Analytical Pipelines

While rules are meant to be broken, I believe having a process flow/framework can often simplify problem solving for a team. We thought a lot about this at Ofgem and came up with a five stage process (Define, Explore, Design, Build, Handover). Maybe the team will open source this progress at some point… in the mean time I’ve written here about the design of analytical pipelines. Get the design process right and you’re more than half way there!
